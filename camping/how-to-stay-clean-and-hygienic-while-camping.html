How to Stay Clean and Hygienic while Camping

Camping is a great way to disconnect from the hustle and bustle of city life and reconnect with nature. However, camping can also mean sacrificing some of the luxuries and conveniences of modern life, such as running water and indoor plumbing. With that in mind, it's important to know how to stay clean and hygienic while camping. Here are some tips to help you stay fresh and clean in the great outdoors.

Bring Plenty of Clean Water

Water is essential for staying clean and hydrated while camping. Make sure to bring plenty of clean water for drinking, cooking, and washing up. You can either bring your own supply of fresh drinking water or bring a portable water filter or water treatment system to purify water from streams and lakes.

Use Biodegradable Soap

When it comes to washing up while camping, it's important to use biodegradable soap that won't harm the environment. Look for soap specifically designed for camping or outdoor use. Avoid using regular household soap, as it can pollute the water and harm wildlife.

Pack Lightweight Towels

Space and weight are at a premium when camping, so it's important to pack lightweight towels that are easy to carry and quick to dry. Microfiber towels are a great option as they are lightweight and absorbent. You can also pack a small washcloth or sponge for washing up.

Practice Leave No Trace

One of the most important aspects of camping is leaving the environment the way you found it. This means practicing Leave No Trace principles, which include packing out all trash and not polluting the environment. When brushing your teeth or washing up, make sure to do so at least 200 feet from any water sources to avoid polluting the water.

Embrace Dry Shampoo and Baby Wipes

When it comes to staying clean while camping, sometimes you have to get a little creative. Dry shampoo is a great option for keeping your hair fresh between showers. Baby wipes are also a great way to freshen up and keep yourself feeling clean between washes.

Bring Hand Sanitizer

Hand sanitizer is a must-have for camping trips to keep yourself and others healthy. Make sure to pack a bottle of hand sanitizer and use it before handling food or eating.

Final Thoughts

Camping can be a great adventure, but it can also mean going without some of the modern amenities we're used to. By following these easy tips on how to stay clean and hygienic while camping, you can ensure a comfortable and enjoyable trip. Remember to pack plenty of clean water, use biodegradable soap, practice Leave No Trace, embrace dry shampoo and baby wipes, bring hand sanitizer, and enjoy the great outdoors!
