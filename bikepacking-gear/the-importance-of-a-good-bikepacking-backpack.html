The Importance of a Good Bikepacking Backpack

Bikepacking is a fantastic way to explore the great outdoors and get some exercise at the same time. However, it's crucial to have the proper gear, including a good bikepacking backpack.

A high-quality backpack is essential for any bikepacking trip. It allows you to carry all of your essentials while keeping them secure and accessible. Without a good backpack, your adventure can quickly turn into a miserable experience.

Why You Need a Good Bikepacking Backpack

A quality bikepacking backpack is designed to meet the unique needs of bikepacking. It should be durable, comfortable, and designed to hold all of your gear securely.

A good backpack should also have plenty of pockets and compartments so that you can organize your gear to quickly locate items when you need them. The backpack should be waterproof, or at the very least water-resistant, to protect your gear from rain and other elements.

What to Look for in a Bikepacking Backpack

When shopping for a bikepacking backpack, there are a few things to look for to ensure you get the right one.

Durability

A good backpack should be durable enough to handle rough terrain and daily wear and tear. Look for a backpack made from high-quality materials such as ripstop nylon or Cordura.

Comfort

Comfort is essential when it comes to bikepacking. Look for a backpack with padded shoulder straps and a padded back. The backpack should also be adjustable to fit your torso.

Capacity

The size of the backpack you need will depend on the length of your trip and the gear you plan to bring. Look for a backpack with enough space to pack all of your essentials, but not so much space that it adds extra weight.

Organization

A good backpack should have plenty of pockets and compartments for organizing your gear. Look for a backpack with multiple pockets, including a separate compartment for your hydration bladder.

Water Resistance

Given that you'll likely be riding through the elements, you need a backpack that is water-resistant, if not waterproof.

Final Thoughts

A good bikepacking backpack is an investment that will last a long time and will make your bikepacking experience much more enjoyable. Take the time to research and find the backpack that best fits your needs. Remember, it's better to have a backpack that is slightly larger than necessary than one that is too small. Happy trails!
