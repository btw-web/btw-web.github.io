Best Bike Touring Bags and Panniers

If you're planning a bike touring adventure, you'll need the right gear to carry your essentials. Backpacks can be uncomfortable and bulky, so most cyclists opt for bags and panniers that attach to their bike. But with so many options available, how do you know which ones are the best? Here are some of our top picks:

1. Ortlieb Back-Roller Classic Panniers

These classic panniers from Ortlieb are a favorite among many bike tourers. They're waterproof, durable, and have a spacious interior that can hold everything you need for a multi-day trip. The QL2.1 mounting system is easy to use and keeps the bags securely in place, even on rough terrain.

2. Blackburn Outpost Elite Frame Bag

Need some extra storage space? The Blackburn Outpost Elite Frame Bag provides a convenient spot for you to stash snacks, tools, or other small items. It fits most bike frames and is made with durable, waterproof materials to keep your things safe and dry.

3. Arkel Dolphin 48 Rear Waterproof Panniers

The Arkel Dolphin 48 Rear Waterproof Panniers are made with heavy-duty materials that can withstand the elements. They have a unique roll-top design that keeps your gear dry, even in heavy rain. Plus, they have a spacious interior that can hold up to 48 liters of gear.

4. Topeak MTX Trunk Bag DXP

If you prefer to keep your storage on the back of your bike rather than on the sides, the Topeak MTX Trunk Bag DXP is a great option. With multiple compartments and a expandable top, this bag can hold everything you need for a day trip or a longer adventure.

5. Brooks England Suffolk Rear Pannier

For a vintage-inspired look, check out the Brooks England Suffolk Rear Pannier. Made with high-quality leather and waxed cotton, these panniers are as stylish as they are functional. They attach easily to your bike and have a spacious interior with multiple pockets.

No matter what your biking style or needs are, there's a bag or pannier out there for you. Make sure to choose one that's durable, weather-resistant, and can carry all your essential gear. Happy touring!
