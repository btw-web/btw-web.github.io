Navigation and GPS Tools for Bike Touring

Bike touring is a fun-filled outdoor adventure that allows you to explore new terrains and challenge yourself physically. However, without effective navigation and GPS tools, you may find it hard to make the most of your bike touring experience. In this article, we’ll explore various navigation and GPS tools for bike touring.

Physical Maps

Physical maps are an essential tool for any bike tourer. They provide a quick overview of the area you are traveling in and the surrounding terrain. Physical maps are crucial when you don’t have an electronic device to rely on, and they come in handy if your electronic maps and GPS fails.

GPS Units

A GPS unit provides accurate and reliable navigation, making it an essential tool for any bike tourer. Some of the features you need to look out for when buying a GPS unit include a large display for easy readability, water-resistant casing to withstand harsh weather conditions, and rechargeable batteries that can last for several hours.

Smartphone Apps

Smartphone apps have revolutionized navigation, making it easier for bike tourers to explore new areas. Mapping applications like Google Maps and Waze offer real-time navigation, allowing you to get updated information about the location you’re traveling in. Additionally, weather apps like AccuWeather can help you track weather conditions and prepare for potential changes in climate.

Cycle Computers

Cycle computers are excellent GPS-enabled devices that offer real-time navigation as well as additional features such as speedometers, odometers, and altimeters. These devices are particularly useful in tracking and monitoring your speed and distance traveled. Some of the most popular cycle computers include Garmin Edge and Lezyne Mega GPS.

Final Thoughts

To make the most of your bike touring experience, it is essential to have reliable navigation and GPS tools. From physical maps to GPS units, smartphone apps to cycle computers, there are various options to choose from. Consider your budget, needs, and preferences when selecting a tool that will best suit your needs. With the right navigation and GPS tools, you can have a seamless and efficient bike touring experience.
