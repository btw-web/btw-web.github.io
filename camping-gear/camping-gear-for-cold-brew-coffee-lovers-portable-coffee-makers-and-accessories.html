Camping Gear for Cold Brew Coffee Lovers: Portable Coffee Makers and Accessories

If you're a fan of cold brew coffee and love spending time outdoors, you don't have to sacrifice your favorite beverage while camping. Thanks to some great portable coffee makers and accessories, it's easy to bring your perfect cup of coffee along for the adventure. Keep reading for some of the best camping gear for cold brew coffee lovers.

Portable Cold Brew Coffee Makers

1. Hario Mizudashi Coffee Pot

The Hario Mizudashi Coffee Pot is a popular option for those who love cold brew coffee. The pot is made of durable glass and has a reusable mesh filter that is easy to clean. You simply add your favorite coffee grounds and water to the pot and let it steep for 8-12 hours in your fridge or cooler. When you're ready to enjoy your coffee, simply pour it out of the pot and into your cup.

2. Primula Cold Brew Coffee Maker

The Primula Cold Brew Coffee Maker is another great option for cold brew coffee lovers. This portable coffee maker is made of borosilicate glass and has a slim design that makes it easy to pack in your camping gear. The coffee maker comes with a stainless steel mesh filter and has an airtight lid that keeps your coffee fresh for up to two weeks.

3. GSI Outdoors Ultralight Java Drip

The GSI Outdoors Ultralight Java Drip is a unique coffee maker that doesn't require any electricity or paper filters. The compact design is ideal for camping and backpacking, and it brews coffee using a simple drip method. Simply place the Java Drip over your cup, add coffee grounds and hot water, and let it drip through the filter into your cup. The Java Drip is made of durable plastic and weighs only 0.4 ounces, making it the perfect camping gear for cold brew coffee lovers.

Accessories

1. Reusable Coffee Filters

If you're using a coffee pot or French press to make cold brew coffee while camping, consider using reusable coffee filters. There are a variety of options available, including metal and cloth filters. Not only are these filters eco-friendly, but they also allow for a better tasting cup of coffee.

2. Insulated Coffee Mug

An insulated coffee mug is a must-have when camping. Not only will it keep your coffee hot, but it also prevents spills and can be used for other beverages as well. Look for a mug with a lid that seals well and has a handle for easy carrying.

3. Portable Milk Frother

If you're a fan of frothy coffee drinks, bring along a portable milk frother. These small devices are battery-operated and can easily be packed in your camping gear. Simply add milk to your coffee, turn on the frother, and enjoy a delicious frothy drink in the great outdoors.

Conclusion

Camping doesn't have to mean sacrificing your favorite beverage. These portable coffee makers and accessories make it easy to enjoy the perfect cup of cold brew coffee while camping. Whether you're using a coffee pot or a drip method, don't forget to bring along reusable coffee filters and your favorite insulated mug for the perfect camping gear for cold brew coffee lovers.
