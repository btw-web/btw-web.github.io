Lightweight and Compact Camping Chairs for Backpackers

When it comes to backpacking, every ounce of weight in our backpacks counts. That's why finding the right balance between comfort and weight is vital. A lightweight and compact camping chair is a must-have for any backpacker who wants to relax and enjoy the view after a long day of hiking.

There are many camping chairs on the market, but not all of them are suitable for backpacking. Below are some of the best lightweight and compact camping chairs for backpackers.

Helinox Chair Zero

The Helinox Chair Zero weighs only 1 pound and packs down to the size of a water bottle, making it a perfect choice for backpackers. Despite its light weight, the Chair Zero is sturdy enough to hold up to 265 pounds. The Chair Zero is designed with a single shock-corded pole structure that makes it easy to set up and take down.

Alite Designs Mayfly Chair

Another excellent choice for backpackers is the Alite Designs Mayfly Chair. This camping chair weighs only 1.6 pounds and packs down small enough to fit in a backpack. The Mayfly Chair has a unique design that allows you to use it as a low chair or a rocking chair.

REI Co-op Flexlite Air Chair

The REI Co-op Flexlite Air Chair is one of the lightest and most compact camping chairs on the market. Weighing only 1 pound, 1 ounce, and packing down to the size of a water bottle, this chair is an excellent choice for backpackers. The Flexlite Air Chair is designed with a single shock-corded pole structure that makes it easy to set up and take down.

Helinox Chair One

The Helinox Chair One is a classic camping chair that has become a favorite among backpackers. The Chair One weighs only 2.1 pounds and packs down to the size of a large water bottle. Despite its light weight, the Chair One is sturdy enough to hold up to 320 pounds.

GCI Outdoor Freestyle Rocker

The GCI Outdoor Freestyle Rocker is an excellent choice for backpackers who want a comfortable camping chair to relax in. Weighing only 12 pounds, the Freestyle Rocker is lightweight enough to carry in a backpack. The chair also features a unique rocking design that allows you to relax while enjoying the great outdoors.

In conclusion, finding the right camping chair for backpacking is essential. The chairs listed above are all excellent choices for backpackers who want to relax and enjoy the view while keeping their pack weight down. Happy camping!
