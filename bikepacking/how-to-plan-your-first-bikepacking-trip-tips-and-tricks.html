How to Plan Your First Bikepacking Trip: Tips and Tricks

Bikepacking is an incredible way to explore the outdoors, get some exercise, and create lasting memories. If you're new to the world of bikepacking, planning your first trip can be overwhelming. Here are some tips and tricks to help you plan your first bikepacking trip.

Decide on Your Destination

The first step in planning a bikepacking trip is to decide on your destination. Will you be exploring a local bike trail, or will you be traveling to a national park?

Choose Your Route

Once you have chosen your destination, the next step is to choose your route. Look for established bikepacking routes in your area or plan your own using mapping software.

Determine Your Gear List

The key to a successful bikepacking trip is to pack light. Determine what gear you will need for your trip and then find the lightest versions of those items.

Test Your Gear

Before you head out on your trip, take the time to test your gear. Set up your tent, cook a meal on your camping stove, and ride with your gear for a few days. This will help you identify any issues before you are out on the trail.

Consider Your Food and Water Needs

When planning your bikepacking trip, consider your food and water needs carefully. While you can carry water with you, there may not always be access to fresh water. Make sure you have a way to filter water if necessary. Pack non-perishable, lightweight foods that will provide the energy you need for your trip.

Learn Basic Bike Maintenance

While on your bikepacking trip, you will need to perform basic bike maintenance. Take the time to learn how to change a tire, adjust brakes, and perform other basic repairs.

Check Weather Conditions

Before you head out on your trip, make sure to check the weather conditions. Pack appropriate gear for the conditions you will be facing.

Be Prepared for Emergencies

Finally, be prepared for emergencies. Bring a first aid kit, a map and compass, and a way to call for help if necessary.

Planning your first bikepacking trip can be overwhelming, but by following these tips and tricks, you can ensure a successful trip. Happy trails!
