Are you planning a bikepacking adventure and looking for the best places to explore in North America? Look no further! We’ve scoured the continent and compiled a list of the top 10 bikepacking destinations that offer breathtaking scenery, thrilling trails, and unique experiences. Whether you’re a seasoned pro or a beginner, these destinations have something to offer every level of rider.

1. Great Divide Mountain Bike Route

The Great Divide Mountain Bike Route is a 2,774-mile trail that runs from Canada to Mexico, covering a diverse range of terrains and landscapes. This route is perfect for those who want a challenging and rewarding adventure, with stunning views of the Rockies and the Great Basin.

2. Trans-America Trail

The Trans-America Trail is a 4,000-mile route that spans the entire width of the United States, from the Atlantic to the Pacific. This trail takes you through some of the most beautiful and remote parts of the country, from the Appalachian Mountains to the Oregon Coast.

3. Olympic Discovery Trail

The Olympic Discovery Trail covers 130 miles of stunning coastal scenery in the Pacific Northwest. This trail takes you through forests, lakes, and mountains, offering breathtaking views of the Olympic National Park.

4. Maah Daah Hey Trail

The Maah Daah Hey Trail is a 142-mile trail that takes you through the rugged badlands of North Dakota. This trail offers a challenging ride through canyons, grasslands, and steep hills, with panoramic views of the prairies and badlands.

5. Katy Trail State Park

The Katy Trail State Park is a 240-mile trail that runs through the heart of Missouri. This trail follows the Missouri River and takes you through charming towns, farmland, and stunning natural beauty.

6. McKenzie River Trail

The McKenzie River Trail is a 26-mile trail that takes you through the lush forests and waterfalls of Oregon’s Cascade Range. This trail offers challenging terrain and breathtaking scenery, with views of the McKenzie River and the Three Sisters Mountains.

7. San Juan Hut Systems

The San Juan Hut Systems offer a unique bikepacking experience, with 215 miles of trails through the rugged San Juan Mountains of Colorado. This route takes you through high alpine terrain, with breathtaking views of the mountains and valleys.

8. White Rim Trail

The White Rim Trail is a 100-mile loop that takes you through the stunning canyons and towering mesas of Utah’s Canyonlands National Park. This trail offers challenging terrain and stunning views of the park’s unique geology and landscapes.

9. The Great Allegheny Passage

The Great Allegheny Passage is a 150-mile trail that takes you through the beautiful Appalachian Mountains, from Pittsburgh, PA to Cumberland, MD. This trail offers stunning scenery and charming towns, with easy terrain that’s perfect for beginners.

10. Copper Canyon Trail

The Copper Canyon Trail is a 200-mile trail that takes you through one of the world’s largest canyon systems in Chihuahua, Mexico. This trail offers breathtaking scenery and challenging terrain, with steep descents and climbs through the canyons.

Conclusion

These top 10 bikepacking destinations in North America offer something for every level of rider, from challenging trails to scenic routes. Whether you’re a seasoned pro or a beginner, these destinations are sure to provide a unique and unforgettable bikepacking experience that you’ll never forget. So grab your bike, pack your bags, and hit the road – adventure is calling!
